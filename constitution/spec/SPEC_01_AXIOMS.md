# SPEC_01 — Axioms

Status: Draft v1
Scope: deterministic replay gate for irreversible AI-assisted code merges.

## Axiom 1 — Irreversible Transition Constraint

No irreversible transition may be constitutionally admitted without deterministic admissibility validation.

## Axiom 2 — Replay Determinism

Identical constitutional lineage, evaluated under the same constitution version, must reproduce identical constitutional conclusions.

## Axiom 3 — Immutable Finality

Finalized constitutional lineage must not be retroactively mutated, reordered, deleted, or reinterpreted.

## Axiom 4 — Operational Non-Authority

Operational execution, runtime success, infrastructure state, or adapter behavior does not constitute constitutional truth.

## Axiom 5 — Replay-Derived Legitimacy

Constitutional legitimacy exists only when admissibility can be deterministically reconstructed from lineage.

## Axiom 6 — Refusal Priority

If admissibility cannot be deterministically established, the transition is NON_CANONICAL.
