# DETERMA Governed Repository Operations

STATUS: CANONICAL
EXPOSURE: INTERNAL_CORE

## Purpose

This document defines the operational governance rules for maintaining the DETERMA repositories over time.

The objective is to:

- preserve continuity
- reduce semantic drift
- enforce disclosure boundaries
- standardize repository evolution
- support long-term maintainability

---

# Canonical Principle

```text
Repository evolution should be governed, not improvised.
```

---

# Operational Categories

## Category 1 — Canonical Maintenance

Tasks:

- maintain source-of-truth integrity
- prevent duplicate canonical ownership
- review superseded material
- preserve lineage references

---

## Category 2 — Disclosure Governance

Tasks:

- review external exposure
- validate sanitization
- verify NotebookLM corpus boundaries
- review public-safe derivatives

---

## Category 3 — Archive Governance

Tasks:

- preserve restricted material continuity
- prevent accidental archive exposure
- maintain relocation mappings
- preserve invention lineage

---

## Category 4 — Diligence Governance

Tasks:

- maintain curated onboarding flows
- review investor-facing material
- validate design partner corpora
- preserve progressive disclosure structure

---

# Repository Change Rules

## Rule 1

Do not introduce new canonical concepts without defining:

- canonical source
- exposure level
- lineage relationship
- sanitization boundary

---

## Rule 2

New public material should:

- support category positioning
- preserve moat boundaries
- align with disclosure doctrine

---

## Rule 3

Deprecated material should:

- remain archived
- preserve references
- avoid remaining active in public corpora

---

# Recommended Governance Cadence

| Activity | Suggested Frequency |
|---|---|
| Disclosure review | Before external exposure |
| Corpus review | Monthly |
| Canonical drift review | Monthly |
| Archive review | Quarterly |
| Public positioning review | Quarterly |

# Canonical Principle

```text
Governance is an ongoing operational process, not a one-time cleanup.
```
