# DETERMA Knowledge Lineage Policy

## Purpose

This document defines how knowledge lineage should be preserved across the DETERMA repositories.

The objective is to:

- preserve reasoning continuity
- support long-term architectural evolution
- reduce semantic drift
- maintain governance integrity

---

# Canonical Principle

```text
Architecture evolves through lineage, not isolated snapshots.
```

---

# Knowledge Lineage

Knowledge lineage includes:

- architectural evolution
- governance evolution
- runtime semantics evolution
- positioning evolution
- threat model evolution
- implementation doctrine evolution

---

# Lineage Preservation Rules

## Rule 1

Do not erase historical reasoning without archival preservation.

---

## Rule 2

When creating sanitized derivatives:

- preserve conceptual continuity
- preserve architectural intent
- avoid semantic distortion

---

## Rule 3

When superseding doctrine:

- reference the previous doctrine
- preserve the evolution chain
- maintain canonical lineage visibility

---

# Public vs Internal Lineage

## Public Layer

Should preserve:

- conceptual evolution
- category framing continuity
- operational reasoning

without exposing moat-critical implementation evolution.

---

## Internal Layer

Should preserve:

- full architectural reasoning
- governance evolution
- sequencing evolution
- implementation lineage
- invention lineage

---

# NotebookLM Rule

NotebookLM corpora should preserve:

- coherent conceptual lineage

while avoiding:

- uncontrolled exposure of restricted historical internals.

---

# Canonical Principle

```text
Understanding deep systems requires understanding how they evolved.
```
