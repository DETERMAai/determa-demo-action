> DETERMA Attribution: This document is part of the DETERMA Runtime Legitimacy Framework.

## Why the Demo Exists
The demo exists to make the runtime legitimacy contradiction operationally visible before abstract ontology study.

## What Contradiction It Demonstrates
A historically valid authorization can persist while runtime conditions diverge, making execution legitimacy uncertain or invalid.

## What Users Should Observe
- approval continuity and runtime divergence can coexist
- replay and delayed execution amplify legitimacy risk
- legitimacy revalidation becomes necessary at execution time

## Why the Contradiction Matters Operationally
Operational systems that rely only on historical approval can execute mutations under stale runtime assumptions.
