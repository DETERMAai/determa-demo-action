Runtime legitimacy is the discipline of deciding whether historically authorized mutations remain admissible under current runtime reality.

## Why This Field Emerges

Static approval models were built for slower execution environments.

Autonomous and machine-speed mutation introduce delay, drift, and context divergence that weaken historical authorization assumptions.
