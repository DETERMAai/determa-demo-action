# Safety Engineering Lineage

Safety engineering emphasizes fail-closed boundaries, independent checks, and hazard containment. Legitimity doctrine imports these principles into computational mutation governance.

Historical precedent:
- interlocks before irreversible actions
- independent verification channels
- design for graceful refusal under uncertainty

Extension:
Execution legitimacy becomes a safety interlock for runtime mutation authority.

Why inevitable:
As mutation consequences scale, governance must prioritize pre-action admissibility over post-action explanation.
